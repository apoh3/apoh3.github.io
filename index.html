HELLO! I'm a PhD student in Computer Science at UMass Amherst. My current research focuses on human computer interaction within the educational learning space. I'm a member of the Advanced Learning Technologies Lab where I study the design, development, and evaluation of tools for enabling teachers to better support their students. I'm also interested in computer science education and have instructed courses at both UMass Amherst and UMass Dartmouth. Before starting my PhD program, I worked at Procter & Gamble as a Data Engineer.
