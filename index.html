ALLISON POH

PhD Student, Computer Science
University of Massachusetts Amherst

Hello! I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Massachusetts Amherst. I am part of the Advanced Learning Technologies Lab where I study the design of teacher support tools. In particular, I am interested in the design, development, and evaluation of teacher dashboards that enable teachers to better support their students. I am also interested in Computer Science education, including computational thinking and higher education pedagogy. In addition to numerous teaching assistantships, I have instructed several college-level courses, including Data Mining & Knowledge Acquisition at UMass Dartmouth and Foundations of Programming at UMass Amherst. Before starting my PhD program, I worked at Procter & Gamble as a Data Engineer.
